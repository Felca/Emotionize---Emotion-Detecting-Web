“Real-time Emotion Recognition: A Bridge to Deeper Understanding in Therapy”

Facial Emotion Recognition

Emotionize is a FER model-based technology that is able to identify and analyze human emotions based on facial expression.

Using elements of computer vision, machine learning, and facial features the AI will interpret emotional state of the patient.

Emotionize can aid in monitoring a patient’s mental health and improve communication between consultant and the patient.
